Welcome to my Homepage


I am currently a final year Data Science post graduate student at Chennai Mathematical Institute, India. I completed my undergraduate studies in Mathematics from Indian Statistical Institute Bangalore, India. I share stuff about what I am learning and doing, on this site.

I'm passionate about the world of AI and data science! With strong proficiency in Python, R, and Julia, I specialize in leveraging machine learning frameworks like Scikit-Learn, Keras, TensorFlow, and PyTorch to solve complex challenges. My expertise in data manipulation and visualization—using libraries like NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Streamlit, ggplot2, and Plotly—helps me transform data into impactful visual stories.

My interest in statistics and machine learning drives me to explore innovative solutions and derive meaningful insights from data. I'm well-versed in SQL for effective data management and analysis, and I'm comfortable using MS Excel, Power BI, and R Shiny to deliver actionable business intelligence.

During my internship at Accenture as a Data and AI intern, I worked on a Supply Chain Optimization project, where I applied my analytical skills to enhance efficiency and improve decision-making. I'm particularly excited about the applications of AI, machine learning, and statistics in addressing real-world challenges and making a difference.
